Accessing snap

    The url for snap is http://snap.berkeley.edu/run

    At this time, we recommend you use the Chrome browser.

    Saving Your Work

    To save your programs, you need to make an account. In the snap browser window, find the cloud-shaped button in the top toolbar on the upper left corner:

    .

    Click it, select the "sign up" option in the menu, and follow the instructions. After creating your account, check your email to get your initial password.
